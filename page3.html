What is the ECOSOC?

The United Nations Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC) is the UN’s powerhouse for tackling global economic, social, and environmental challenges. It links governments, NGOs, and experts to design sustainable solutions that change lives worldwide.

In this committee, countries collaborate on:

  • Eradicating poverty and hunger
  • Promoting education, health, and well-being
  • Advancing climate action and green economies
  • Ensuring inclusive and sustainable development

ECOSOC crafts the blueprint for a fairer, greener, and more prosperous world.

What do YOU do in MUN as an ECOSOC delegate?

You advocate your country’s developmental goals and build global partnerships to drive change.

Your mission?

  • Promote policies for economic and social equity
  • Collaborate across regions and cultures
  • Debate issues through a lens of sustainability
  • Create actionable resolutions that serve humanity

It’s not just policy-making — it’s building a better tomorrow for generations to come.
